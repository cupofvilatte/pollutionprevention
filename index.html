Help Us Stop Pollution In National Parks

Our Mission

Our mission at Pollution Prevention is to try and mitigate pollution in national parks around the Idaho area, as well as encourage the end of pollution around the world. We are accomplishing this via our Pollution Prevention app.

Get Involved

YOU can get involved by downloading our pollution prevention app, linked above, which will help keep you accontable with littering and encourage you to pick up garbage whenever you in a national park. It also gives data about how pollution is affecting our parks and what we can do to stop it. There is no downsides to you downloading it as it is free and only encourages you to help out with cleaning up the parks.
